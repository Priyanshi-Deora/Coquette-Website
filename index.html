How to be coquette:

♡ Highly coded feminine clothing like dresses and skirts are key pieces to nail this look. Choose items that accentuate the female form, while still feeling innocent and classy. Items with bardot or sweetheart necklines and off the shoulder sleeves are delicate yet tasteful.
♡ Wearing lace detailed aesthetic tops and adding ribbons, doing embroidery, altering the silhouette are ideal for this style.
♡ Creating your own Coquette-style accessories. ♡ Mixing and matching different pieces you already own are perfect!

The history of being coquette:

♡ Originating in the 18th century, particularly during the Rococo period, the coquette aesthetic became associated with feminine, cute personalities or clothing.
♡ Having originated in Italian theatrical traditions, the coquette figure became an emblem of French femininity in Ancien Régime comedy where she represented the emerging image of the girly, cute, firty type of womem.
♡ Los Angeles-based stylist Marisa Ledford tells PEOPLE, “The coquette fashion trend is Gen Z's take on flirty, soft, hyper-feminine style which references the Victorian Regency era."
